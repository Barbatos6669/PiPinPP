◆ BUSYWAIT_THRESHOLD_US

constexpr long pipinpp::BUSYWAIT_THRESHOLD_US = 100
constexpr

Threshold for switching from sleep to busy-wait (microseconds)

Below this threshold, we busy-wait for precision. Above this threshold, we use clock_nanosleep() to yield CPU.

Tuning notes:

  • Too small: No CPU savings
  • Too large: Increased timing jitter
  • 100Âµs is empirically optimal for Pi 4/5
